This guideline is meant to provide both parties (sellers and buyers) with a condition standard for trading a book. Sellers must follow this guideline when posting (and later selling) their book. If the condition is not met, buyers have the right to return the book back to the seller for a full refund (unless the buyer used the book prior to the return). If found that the seller repeatedly violated the guideline, the seller's id will be removed from the system.

NEW: A brand-new, unused, unread copy in perfect condition. Perhaps, the copy that the bookstore won't buy back because it is an old edition.
LIKE NEW: A copy that has been read, but remains in excellent condition. It should not have any highlights and nor should it have any damages as a whole. It must include all of the original materials (such as CD).
GOOD: A copy that has been read, but remains in clean condition. All pages are intact, and the cover is intact (including dust cover, if applicable). The spine may show signs of wear. Pages can include limited notes and highlighting. May not include some of the original materials (such as CD).
ACCEPTABLE: A readable copy. All pages are intact and may include elaborate markings or highlightings, though not obscuring the original text. The book may show signs of water damage. May not include all of the original materials (such as CD).
